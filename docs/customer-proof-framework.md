# Customer Proof Framework

This framework defines how NeutralAI can publish customer proof without inventing outcomes or overstating evidence.

## Scope

- Related issue: [#77](https://github.com/nazifsohtaoglu/neutralai-website/issues/77)
- Applies to: homepage trust sections, trust-center content, sales-facing public case-study copy.
- Does not replace private legal/compliance approval workflows.

Assumption: Public website copy must remain conservative unless each proof item has a documented source and approval.

## Allowed Public Proof Types

1. Named customer quote only after explicit written approval from the customer and internal Legal sign-off.
2. Anonymized case study with non-identifying language and approval from Compliance and Legal.
3. Measured pilot summary only when methodology, timeframe, and limitations are documented.
4. Usage-count claim only when sourced from a repeatable analytics query with owner sign-off.
5. Security review artifact summary only when wording is approved by Security and Legal.
6. Product benchmark evidence only when source, scope, and limitations are linked.

## Required Approval Gates

Every proof asset must pass all gates below before publication:

1. Source validation: product, analytics, or evidence owner confirms source integrity.
2. Legal review: wording and attribution are approved for public use.
3. Compliance review: claim boundaries and regulatory language are validated.
4. Marketing review: voice and clarity are aligned with public positioning.
5. Final owner sign-off: Product Marketing approves publication.

## Safe Wording Rules

- State scope, timeframe, and conditions for each proof claim.
- Use "can", "supports", or "under review" when outcomes depend on customer deployment choices.
- Keep benchmark claims labeled as product benchmarks unless independently validated.
- Include caveats for NDA-only artifacts and non-public supporting details.

## Prohibited Wording

- No invented customer logos, testimonials, or usage numbers.
- No blanket compliance guarantees (for example, unconditional HIPAA or GDPR compliance claims).
- No "trusted by X customers" counts without a validated source and approval record.
- No claims of independent validation unless a third-party report is publicly available and approved.

## Publication Workflow

1. Open or update the linked issue with the proof candidate and owner.
2. Attach source evidence reference and draft public wording.
3. Complete approval gates and record decision in the issue.
4. Update website content and related content tests in the same PR.
5. If proof is not approved, keep conservative placeholder copy and document the decision.

## Current Public Posture (2026-05-15)

- The website intentionally avoids customer logos/testimonials and unverified usage counts.
- Trust content is limited to verified product evidence, benchmark context, and explicit caveats.
- Customer proof slots remain conservative until an approved asset clears this framework.
